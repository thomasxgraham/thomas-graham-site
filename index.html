How I work

At each phase of the product lifecycle I take intentional steps to keep the user's voice present — not as an afterthought, but as part of how the work moves forward.

Step 01

Reframe the question

Most teams already know the symptom. I work to find the actual problem, which is rarely what was first described.

Step 02

Validate the signal

If something important appears in the data, I test it from different angles until I'm confident it's showing what it appears to show.

Step 03

Stay until something moves

Research that stops at the deliverable is only half the job. I bring recommendations, stay in the conversation, and follow through.
