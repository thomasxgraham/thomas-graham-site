UX Researcher · Product Thinker

I care about making difficult moments easier.

The work I’m drawn to has a shape to it. Stakes are real, the user is already under pressure, and the team can’t afford to confuse “we understand them” with “we know what to do.”

When the decision matters, sloppy research can be costly. The job is to leave less ambiguity behind than you found. Keep the questions honest, pressure-test the data, and make sure the team is reading it the same way before they act.

Perspective

I’ve worked every side of where research lands.

UX research doesn’t sit at one end of the product lifecycle. I’ve been lucky to have a seat at the discovery, design, development, and governance tables. That’s why I know what makes research land in a deck no one reopens, and what makes it shift what actually gets built.

Working style

How I show up on teams

I do my best work with teams that genuinely want to know what’s true, even when the answer reshapes the plan.

I prefer being embedded with a team to operating at a distance from one.

Working with me

You can count on me to be the one asking questions, and to keep annoyingly asking until we know what we’re actually dealing with.
