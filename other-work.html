Pulse Nightclub Fundraiser

TL;DR

After the Pulse nightclub shooting, my team and I turned a quick design response into a volunteer-led fundraiser that raised more than $80,000 for the victims’ families.

The fundraiser started as a shirt concept for a single vigil and turned into a citywide effort. Design, production, packaging, distribution: all of it had to move fast and stay careful.
